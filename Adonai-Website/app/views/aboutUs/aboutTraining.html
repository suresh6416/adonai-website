About Training

About Us

Adonai Technologies has established training division in Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh, India in 2017. Our training division offers high quality training both for fresher and young graduates who are in their final semester. Our Company also provides ON JOB TRAINING for employees to enrich knowledge and careers of young graduate engineers.

Team

We employ a core team of exceptional individuals and draw on a wide array of professional associates and support personnel on an as-needed basis. We have a diverse human resource capacity that consists of individuals that have worked on international, national and local levels with proven track records of delivery in the education, training and development programmers to the state, private and non-governmental sectors.
Within our pool of professionals are people that have worked extensively in all corners of Andhra Pradesh. It should be noted that our staff and Associates come from a host of backgrounds including national and local government, business and civil society organizations.

Our Principle

We pursue the highest quality in all our training intervention by ensuring that our materials are relevant and unit standard aligned; our facilitators are experts in their field and experienced ETD professionals and that our approach to leaning is both visionary and innovative.

Our Unique Concept

Technologies used in the IT industry have drastically changed from last few years. We always focus on the Latest Technologies which assures a bright career for a fresh graduate. We offer a Full Time 14 week course in which student will be trained in the latest technologies of his preference like MEAN stack Development, Internet of Things, Data Science, Cyber Security and Block Chain Technologies.
